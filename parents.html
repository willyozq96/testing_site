What is Show & Tell?

Show & Tell is an interactive learning tool where your child can explore and pick up new words. Children learn by observing, listening and exploring at a young age. It is essential for children to be curious, interested, engaged and motivated once they start school.

In 3 easy steps - choose, upload and click, your child can benefit from learning new words and pronouncing them, which aids them in better reading, writing and communication skills. Show & Tell can also act as an alternative material, aside from the traditional books and formal lessons at school, to support your child's learning.

Child Development

With the use of Show & Tell, parents can support your child's learning by stimulating conversations. By engaging in conversations with your kids, it helps to develop a strong bond, while supporting their cognitive development and improving their social and interaction skills.

Show & Tell provides a window of opportunity to support your child's learning by building great knowledge from a young age. Since children are naturally intrigued, they can absorb new information quickly with the text and audio aid that we provide - Visual and Auditory learning styles.

As parents have a tremendous influence on their children, parents can understand how a child learns and improve on how to teach them as they grows. Thus, creating a great potential for the impact of positive development influences.
